Population Distribution Visualizations

This website provides interactive visualizations showing population distribution across different countries. Using a slider, you can see what percentage of a country's population lives in specific areas, helping to understand population density and distribution patterns.

Each visualization allows you to:

  • Adjust a slider to see different population percentage thresholds
  • View which areas contain specific percentages of the total population
  • Understand population concentration in urban centers versus rural areas
